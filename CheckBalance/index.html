Physics Concepts

Torque

The rotational force that causes objects to rotate. Calculated as force × distance from pivot point.

Lever Principle

A lighter weight placed farther from the fulcrum can balance a heavier weight closer to the fulcrum.

Center of Mass

The point where the mass of an object is concentrated. For balance, this should be above the fulcrum.

Equilibrium

A state where all forces and torques are balanced, resulting in no net movement.
