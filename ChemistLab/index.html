How to Play

  • Move your player (yellow circle) around the lab using the arrow buttons.
  • Collect elements (blue squares) by moving onto them.
  • Elements respawn randomly! You can collect multiple copies of each element.
  • Use equipment (orange squares) to combine elements into compounds.
  • Create required compounds to unlock the exit (purple square).
  • Combine elements by standing next to equipment and clicking the green combine button.
