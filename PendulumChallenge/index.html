Current Challenge

Swing the pendulum bob through the green target zone at the bottom of its swing.

Pendulum Physics Formulas:
• Period: T = 2π√(L/g) (Time for one swing)
• Speed at bottom: v = √(2gL(1-cosθ))
• Potential Energy: PE = mgh
• Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv²

Target Position: Center bottom area

Physics Tip: Pendulum period depends only on length, not mass!

How to Solve Pendulum Challenges

  • Step 1: Look at the target zone location (green rectangle).
  • Step 2: Adjust pendulum length for swing speed (longer = slower).
  • Step 3: Set release angle for swing height (larger = higher).
  • Step 4: Mass affects energy but not swing speed (physics fact!).
  • Step 5: Click "Release Pendulum" to test your settings.
  • Step 6: If you miss, adjust and try again. You have 5 attempts per level.
  • Key Concept: At the bottom of swing, all potential energy becomes kinetic energy!
