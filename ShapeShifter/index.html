How to Play

  1. Study the target pattern on the left grid
  2. Select a shape from the palette below
  3. Click on cells in the right grid to place your selected shape
  4. Match the pattern exactly - both shape type and color
  5. Click "Check Match" to see if you got it right
  6. Complete all levels before time runs out!

Tip: You can click on a placed shape to remove it!
